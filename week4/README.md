> **Theme:** Convert. Negotiate. Close.

## 🎯 The North Star for Week 4

By Sunday May 31 you will have:

1. **At least 1 active offer or final-round interview** (or a clear, named timeline for one)
2. A **negotiation playbook** ready for when the offer arrives
3. A **conference talk proposal** submitted somewhere (Bay Area Women in Data, PAPIs, ODSC, etc.)
4. A **provisional patent** filing path decided (Tech Transfer route OR self-file OR drop)
5. A **"Month 1 in review"** post on LinkedIn — the closing-the-loop post that makes the whole campaign feel inevitable in hindsight
6. A **90-day forward roadmap** so that whatever the Week 4 outcome, Week 5 has direction

**The metric:** the answer to "what's next?" is concrete and named — not "more posts."
