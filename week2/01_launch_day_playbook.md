## 🚨 Failure modes to watch for

1. **You post and immediately panic-edit** — DON'T. LinkedIn penalizes edits in the first hour.
2. **Someone trolls or critiques harshly** — reply once, professionally, then mute. Don't get drawn into a debate.
3. **Recruiter doesn't respond by Friday** — that's normal. Don't double-DM. Wait until next Tuesday.
4. **You hit a viral moment and freeze** — stay grounded. Reply to comments at the same pace. Don't suddenly post 3 follow-ups.
5. **You realize there's a typo / broken link** — small typo, leave it. Broken link, edit at the end of Day 2 (not in the golden hour).
