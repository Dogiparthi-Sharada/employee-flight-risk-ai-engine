## 🚨 Anti-patterns to avoid

- ❌ Tracking impressions instead of conversations (vanity metric)
- ❌ Updating the tracker but never *acting* on what's in it
- ❌ Letting "Awaiting reply" rot for >2 weeks without a polite nudge
- ❌ Forgetting to log a conversation because it felt "casual"
- ❌ Maintaining 4 different trackers in 4 different tools (pick one and stick with it)

---

## 🛠️ Tooling recommendations

| Tool | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Notion** | Best overall — relational databases, mobile, free |
| **Google Sheets** | Lowest friction if you're already in Google ecosystem |
| **Airtable** | If you want forms + DB + automations |
| **Trello / Linear** | If you prefer kanban over tables |
| **A pinned Apple Note** | Underrated — fastest entry, syncs everywhere |

**Don't overthink the tool.** A messy Google Sheet you actually update beats a perfect Notion you never open.
