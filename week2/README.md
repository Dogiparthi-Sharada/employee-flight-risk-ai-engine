### Tuesday May 12 — 🚀 LAUNCH DAY

**8:25 AM PT:** Coffee. Re-read the FINAL CUT post in `LINKEDIN_POST.md` once. Don't tweak.

**8:30 AM PT:** Hit Post.

**8:31 AM PT:** Drop the prepared first-comment immediately. (See `01_launch_day_playbook.md`)

**8:35 AM PT:** Post the same content as a fresh post on:
- Twitter/X (thread version — see `02_twitter_thread.md`)
- Hacker News (Show HN — only if you want technical traffic, not jobs)

**8:30–10:30 AM PT (THE GOLDEN 2 HOURS):** Reply to *every* comment within 5 minutes. LinkedIn's algorithm weights early-window engagement heavily.

**10:30 AM PT:** Send DM to Lumentum recruiter:
> *"Wanted to share what I've been building — open-sourced AttriSense this morning, paper preprint went live last week. Would love any feedback if it's useful for our next conversation."*
> + Link to the LinkedIn post.

**11:30 AM PT:** Send 5 cold-but-warm DMs to target-company employees (template in `03_cold_dm_templates.md`).

**12:30 PM PT:** Post the Tweet thread. Engage X for 30 min.

**4:00 PM PT:** Drop the second LinkedIn comment ("update — really humbled by the responses…")

**Evening:** No more posting. Reply to comments only. Eat dinner. Rest.
